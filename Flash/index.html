Instructions

  • Select your BlockBoy version above
  • Choose the firmware version from the dropdown
  • Connect your BlockBoy via USB-C
  • Click "Flash Firmware"
  • Select the correct COM port
  • Wait for the flash to complete (~1-2 min)

Note: The firmware will be overwritten. ROMs and saves on the microSD card are not affected.
