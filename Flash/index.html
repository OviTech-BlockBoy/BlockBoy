BlockBoy Flasher

Flash the latest firmware to your BlockBoy

Firmware version:

Instructions

  • Select your BlockBoy version above
  • Choose the firmware version
  • Connect using a USB-A to USB-C cable
  • Click the green Flash button
  • Select the correct COM port
  • Wait ~1-2 minutes

Important

The firmware will be overwritten. ROMs and saves on the microSD card are not affected.

USB-C to USB-C cables are not supported. Use a USB-A to USB-C cable.
